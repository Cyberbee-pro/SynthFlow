# 🌿 SynthFlow

**SynthFlow** is a high-performance, open-source music streaming ecosystem built to give the music back to the listener. Inspired by the "01" leek aesthetic, it offers a seamless, high-fidelity audio experience completely free from ad interruptions.

Unlike mainstream platforms, **SynthFlow** prioritizes high-quality playback and provides full manual control over your library, bypassing restrictive corporate algorithms for a truly independent listening environment.

---

### **🚀 Key Features**

* **Ad-Free Experience**: No interruptions, no corporate bloat—just your music.
* **High-Fidelity Audio**: Optimized for premium sound quality across all devices.
* **Full User Control**: You manage your library and playback settings without algorithmic interference.
* **"Leek-Olive" Design**: A custom, "01"-inspired aesthetic based on organic gradients.
* **Multi-Platform Native Core**: Dedicated builds for web, desktop, and mobile.
* **Satisfying Session Algorithm**: A custom-coded algorithm designed to sequence tracks for the most satisfying transitions and flow during long listening sessions.

---

### **🛠 Technical Stack**

**SynthFlow** utilizes a diverse, high-performance stack to ensure a native feel on every device:

* **Web**: Next.js & Express
* **Mobile (Android)**: Flutter
* **Desktop (Linux/Windows)**: Qt6
* **macOS**: Development Pending
* **iOS**: Not planned
